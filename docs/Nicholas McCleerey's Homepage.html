I am an Assistant Professor at Purdue University, West Lafayette.

Before that, I was a postdoc at the University of Michigan, Ann Arbor, working with Mattias Jonsson. I did my graduate studies at Northwestern University, working under the supervision of Valentino Tosatti.

I study complex geometry.

CV (updated: Jan. '25)

This year, I am co-organizing the Geometry and Geometric Analysis Seminar at Purdue, with Lvzhou Chen. If you are interested in speaking, feel free to contact me.

I am a mentor for the Math Alliance. Feel free to reach out!
