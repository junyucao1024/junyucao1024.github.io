The following errata have been identified in [arXiv:2603.04802v1](https://arxiv.org/abs/2603.04802v1) and will be addressed in the next version.

* **Cross-referencing Errors:** Due to a `cleveref` labeling issue, the following labels appear incorrectly throughout the paper:
  * **"Theorem 3.3"** should read **"Definition 3.3"**.
  * **"Theorem 4.1"** should read **"Definition 4.1"**.

* **"Hölder-continuous"** in **"Remark 0.7"** should read **"Hölder continuous"**.
